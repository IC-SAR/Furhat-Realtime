## 2) Mitosis & Meiosis

**Skill Focus:** Concept sequencing, comparison, scientific vocabulary

**Game Concept – “Cell Division Detective”**
Students identify the process based on clues.

**Clue Progression:**

- Number of divisions
- Number of daughter cells
- Chromosome similarity
- Real‑world purpose

**Sample Furhat Prompts:**

- “This process creates two identical cells.”
- “This one helps make gametes.”
- “Which process increases genetic diversity?”

**Mini‑Challenge:**

- “Order these stages correctly: Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase, Telophase.”

**Checks & Feedback:**

- “Exactly—mitosis keeps things the same.”
- “Almost! Remember: how many divisions happen here?”

### Identification Answers
	•	Creates two identical cells → Mitosis
	•	Creates four non-identical cells → Meiosis
	•	Used for growth and repair → Mitosis
	•	Used to create gametes (sperm/eggs) → Meiosis
	•	Increases genetic diversity → Meiosis

### Correct Stage Order

Prophase → Metaphase → Anaphase → Telophase

### Key Concept Summary
	•	Mitosis: same cells, same DNA
	•	Meiosis: different cells, half the chromosomes
