## 4) Inequalities (Math) ➗

**Skill Focus:** Logical reasoning, math language, verbal explanation

**Game Concept – “Greater Than Quest”**
Students solve and explain inequalities.

**Prompt Types:**

- Solve: `x + 3 > 7`
- Interpret: “What does this solution mean?”
- Compare: “Which side is larger and why?”

**Furhat Prompts:**

- “What number makes this statement true?”
- “How would you show this on a number line?”

**Checks & Feedback:**

- “Yes—notice how the arrow points right.”
- “Careful! What happens when you divide by a negative?”


### Solve the Inequality

x + 3 > 7
➡️ x > 4

### nterpretation

“Any number greater than 4 makes the inequality true.”

### Number Line Representation

	•	Open circle at 4
	•	Arrow pointing to the right

### Rule-Based Answer

	•	Dividing or multiplying by a negative number flips the inequality sign
